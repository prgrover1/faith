How it works

From spreadsheet to
vendor order in seconds

Faith sits quietly on top of your existing inventory tracking and springs into action when stock dips.

01

Connect your spreadsheet

Link your Excel or Google Sheets inventory file. Faith reads your product names, current stock, and safety stock thresholds — no reformatting needed.

02

Faith monitors silently

As stock levels change, Faith tracks them in real time. When any SKU hits its safety threshold, Faith immediately identifies the right vendor and drafts a reorder email.

03

You review & approve

You get a one-click approval card for each draft. Review, tweak if needed, and hit send — or let Faith learn your preferences and automate further over time.

Features

Everything a small business needs,
nothing it doesn't

Spreadsheet-native sync

No migration, no new software to learn. Faith reads directly from your existing Excel or Google Sheets file structure, column names and all.

Real-time stock alerts

Faith watches every SKU continuously. Dashboard alerts surface the moment stock crosses a safety threshold — categorized by urgency.

AI-drafted vendor emails

Faith writes personalized, professional reorder emails for each vendor — including product specs, quantities, and preferred delivery windows.

One-click approvals

Every draft lands in your approval queue. You stay in control — review, edit, or approve and send with a single click. No email client needed.
