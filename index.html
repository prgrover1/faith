How it works

From spreadsheet to
vendor order in seconds

Three steps. Zero friction. Faith does the heavy lifting.

01

Connect your sheet

Link your Excel or Google Sheets file. Faith reads product names, current stock, and safety thresholds — no reformatting required.

02

Faith watches silently

As stock levels change, Faith monitors continuously. The moment any SKU crosses its safety threshold, Faith identifies the vendor and drafts the email.

03

You approve & send

Review Faith's draft, tweak if needed, and approve with one click. The vendor gets a professional reorder email — without you writing a word.
